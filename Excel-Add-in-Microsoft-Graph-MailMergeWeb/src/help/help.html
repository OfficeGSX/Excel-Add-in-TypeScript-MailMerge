Step 1: Set up your email template
  1. Add a folder to your Outlook inbox called "Mail Merge Templates."
  2. In the folder, write an email to use as a template.
  3. Use tags in your template to represent text that will change based on who the recipient is.
  4. Save the email as a draft.
Step 2: Fill out your mailing list in Excel
  1. After selecting a template, we generate a table in your Excel spreadsheet for you to fill in your recipients' information.
  2. Enter recipients' email addresses in the EmailAddress column.
  3. Add recipients' information to all cells that have an email address and a column header.
Step 3: Preview and send your email
  1. After using the add-in to merge your email template with your recipients' information, you will have a personalized email to send out to each of your recipients.
